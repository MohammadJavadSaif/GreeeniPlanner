# ◉ GreeniPlanner

**Plant a goal. Watch it branch. Don't water it with vague intentions.**

---

## What is this thing

Most goal-tracking apps want you to feel like you're filling out a tax form. GreeniPlanner wants you to feel like you're running a small, well-organized, slightly intimidating garden.

You plant a **Long-term Goal** at the top. It grows **Medium-term** and **Short-term** branches. Those branches grow **Tasks**. Tasks grow **Sub-tasks**. Five levels, top to bottom, no skipping steps — because "I'll just wing it" is how a 10-year goal quietly turns into a browser tab you never close.

```
🌳 Long-term Goal
 └── 🌿 Medium-term Goal
      └── 🌱 Short-term Goal
           └── ✅ Task
                └── └ Sub-task
```

No accounts. No cloud. No "upgrade to Pro to see your own data." Everything lives in one file, on your machine, under your control.

---

## The two rooms in this house

### 🗂️ Goals — the long game
This is the tree. Click **+ New Goal** to plant a Long-term, Medium-term, or Short-term goal (Tasks and Sub-tasks get added *inside* the tree, not from the top — they need a parent branch to grow from, same as in nature).

Every goal gets:
- A name (required — vague goals don't get a slot)
- A description (optional, but future-you will thank present-you)
- A timeframe — a number plus Years or Months
- A date, picked from the in-app calendar, if you want one

Tasks additionally get a **priority**: High, Medium, or Chill. Not everything is a fire.

Click any card to expand it. The stats up top quietly count everything for you — long-term, medium-term, short-term, tasks, sub-tasks — so you always know how big your forest has gotten.

### ☀️ Daily Tasks — the right-now
This is not the tree. This is today.

A date strip up top lets you flip through days like a desk calendar — jump a week at a time, hit **Today** to snap back, or use the 📅 button to leap anywhere on the calendar. Whatever day you're on is the day you're planning.

Each daily task gets one of four moods, and they are not subtle:

| Priority | Vibe |
|---|---|
| 🔴 **Kill it!** | This is the thing. Today. |
| 🟡 **Mid Slash!** | Important, not on fire. |
| 🟢 **Final Rip!** | The last push to finish something. |
| 🔵 **Chill Bro!** | Low stakes. Breathe. |

You can also link a daily task back to a Task from your Goals tree — so "answer client emails" can quietly trace back to "Launch the thing" three levels up, and you remember *why* you're doing it at 9am on a Tuesday.

## Things worth knowing

- **🌓 Light / Dark** — top-right corner. Pick your contrast.
- **Everything autosaves.** There is no save button because there is nothing to forget to click.
- **Deleting a branch deletes what's under it.** GreeniPlanner will always ask first, but it won't lie to you about the size of what you're cutting.
- **Your data lives in this file's local storage.** Don't move or rename the app file once you start using it — it's less "cloud sync," more "the data is genuinely, only, right here."
